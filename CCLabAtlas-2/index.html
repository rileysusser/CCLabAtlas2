Nefaroflora

Scientific Name:

Florin Osmosa

Discovered by / at:

Riley Susser, 2:58a.m. 6 March 2024

Family:

Florin Osmosa belongs to a unique family of plants that exist only on the surface of Mars. This plant family's highly evolved huntly skills has played a crucial factor in the limited possibilities of life on Mars throughout history.

Habitat:

Found in remote region on Mars. Requires little water, feeds off of innocent prey.

Appearance:

Alluring, beautiful appearance from a distance. Evolved to capture other organism's eyes bringing them in closer to the dangerous reality of its hunting capabilities.

Key Characteristics:

  • Preys off of the innocent
  • Appears harmless
  • Likes to deceive prey
  • Grows more powerful and beautiful the more it preys

What we know so far about Nefaroflora? Nefaroflora is a unique creature combined with both real plant elements and elements from space. It utilizes its floral patterns to lure in prey, and later captures them in the dark hole of space in which they exist, shocking them with venomous supernatural elements introducing foreign radioactive elements into their body.
